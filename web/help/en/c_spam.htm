You are able to set how to block, delivery spam messages and filtering levels. You also can manage unblocked or blocked mail addresses.

  1. Click [Options > Spam Management].
  2. Set a spam policy as well as unblocked and blocked mail addresses.
    • Default Settings
    • Auto Spam Filtering
    • Manage Unblocked/Blocked Mail Addresses
  3. Click Save once you finish setting.

 
Default Settings

Spam Filtering Policy

You are able to set a policy to prevent spam messages from entering your mailbox.

  • Auto Spam Filtering Control Use
    If incoming messages are regarded as spam, follow system rules.
     
  • Auto Spam Filtering Control Not Use
    You don't use Auto Spam Filtering Control.
     
  • Receive all messages
    Receive all incoming messages.
     
  • Block all messages but unblocked lists
    Do not receive any of incoming messages except messages from unblocked lists. Your contacts are not blocked regardless of this filtering policy.
     

Spam Message Delivery

Designate what action will be done once spam messages are incoming.

  • Deliver to Spam
  • Deliver to Trash
  • Delete forever

 

 
Auto Spam Filtering

You can set a spam filtering level. According to the level, messages will be blocked or allowed.

[NOTE]
Auto Spam Filtering is only available when Spam Filtering Policy is set as Auto Spam Filtering Control Use.

  • Low Level (Obvious spam messages will be filtered.)
  • Med Level (Filter out normal spam messages.)
  • High Level (Filter out most of spam messages, Recommended)

 

 
Manage Unblocked/Blocked Mail Addresses

You can add or delete unblocked and blocked addresses.

Add Mail Addresses

[NOTE]
Addresses in your contacts are not blocked even though they are in Blocked Addresses.


To add mail addresses to Unblocked or Blocked Addresses, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [Options > Spam Management].
  2. Type an address or a domain into a text box in Unblocked Addresses or Blocked Addresses.
    (eg) daou_user@daou.co.kr, @daou.co.kr
  3. Click Add.
  4. Click Save.
  5. A completion message will be prompted. Click OK.

Delete Mail Addresses

To delete mail addresses from Unblocked or Blocked Addresses, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [Options > Spam Management].
  2. Select mail addresses from either Unblocked Addresses or Blocked Addresses you'd like to delete.
  3. Click Delete.
  4. Click Save.
  5. A completion message will be prompted. Click OK.

Search Mail Addresses

To search for a mail address from Unblocked or Blocked Addresses, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [Options > Spam Management].
  2. Type a keyword into a text box in Unblocked Addresses or Blocked addresses.
  3. Click Search.
  4. Mail addresses you've searched for will be displayed with highlight if they exist.
