Recently sent
Manage Recently Sent

Recently Sent shows the recipients of your most recently sent messages. If you send an email to a group, it will not be saved to recently sent lists.
Manage Recently sent

It is available to search or delete Email addresses on the menu [Options > Recently sent].

Search an Email address

Search an Email address in the recently sent lists.
  1. Click [Options > Recently sent].
  2. Input an Email address into a Recently sent lists box.
  3. Click Search.

Delete Email addresses

Delete Email addresses from the recently sent lists.
  1. Click [Options > Recently sent].
  2. Select at least more than an Email address to delete from the Recently sent lists.
    Or Click All.
  3. Click Delete.
  4. When it is finished, Click Save.
  5. A completion message will appear. Click OK.
