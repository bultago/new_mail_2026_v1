You can view status of a reservation for assets and use official assets by making a reservation.

[NOTE]
Assets are managed in [Services > Scheduler Settings > Official Asset Management] in admin page. If there is no asset registered, Reserve menu cannot be displayed.

 
Make a reservation

You can view registered assets and reserve available assets. To make a reservation, follow the steps below.

  1. Click an asset you'd like to reserve in [Calendar > Reserve assets].
  2. Click empty area of the calendar. Add an Event window will be prompted.
  3. Enter detail information of the event.

Change a reservation

You can change a reservation as long as you made it.

  1. Click an asset you'd like to change in [Calendar > Reserve assets].
  2. Click the event you'd like to change. Modify an Event will be prompted. Click Modify.
  3. Click Modify once you set details of the event button.

 
Delete a reservation

You can delete a reservation as long as you made it.

  1. Click an asset you'd like to change in [Calendar > Reserve assets].
  2. Click Delete.
  3. The confirmation message will be popped up. Click OK.

 
View a reservation

You can view reservation status on a weekly and asset basis.

  1. Click an asset you'd like to change in [Calendar > Reserve assets].
  2. You can view a reservation status.
    • If you click an event, details of the event will be displayed.
    • You can simply make a reservation by click empty area of the calendar.
    • Go to another week by clicking ◀ or ▶ button next to the date on the top of the screen.
