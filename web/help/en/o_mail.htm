You can send an email quick and easily in Organization.

  • Send Email to Specific Members – Send an email after you choose recipients.
  • Send Email to All – Send an email to members to those who have the same title or class. You also can send an email to who are in the same department at once.

 
Send Email to Specific Members

To send an email to specific members, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [Organization].
  2. Select a department.
  3. Select members who you'd like to email and click Send message.
  4. Compose window will be popped up filled with the members you selected as recipients.
  5. Write an email and click Send.

[NOTE]
Refer to Compose Message to learn more about how to send an email.

 
Send Email to All

To send an email to those who have the same title, same class or who are in the same department, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [Organization].
  2. Select a department.
  3. Click Send to all on the Basic tab menu.
  4. Select Send by title, Send by class, Current org only.
    • include sub-org – Click if you'd like to email to members who are under the department you selected.
  5. Click Send message.
  6. Compose window will be popped up filled with the members you selected as recipients.
  7. Write an email and click Send.

[NOTE]
Refer to Compose Message to learn more about how to send an email.
