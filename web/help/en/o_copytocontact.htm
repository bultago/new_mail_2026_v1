You can copy organization member's contacts to your personal contact simply by clicking.

 
Copy to contacts

To copy, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [Organization].
  2. Click department name of the organization tree on the left.
  3. Check members you'd like to copy and click Copy on the Basic tab menu.
  4. The contacts will be copied to your personal contacts. Member information will be inserted into your personal contacts matched like below.

[Match member information with personal contacts]
