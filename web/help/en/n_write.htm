Write Notes

To write a note, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [Note] on the right top.
  2. Click Compose on the top of the left frame. Compose window will be popped up.
  3. Enter recipients. You can specify up to 10 recipients.
    • To myself – If you'd like to send the note to yourself, check it.
    • Save into Sent – The note you're writing will be saved in Sent.
  4. Enter contents. You can enter up to 400 characters in a text format.
  5. Click Send on top, once you finish writing it.
    • Send via email - If you click this button, Compose window will be popped up. Recipients and contents will be filled automatically.
  6. The completion message will be prompted. Click OK.
