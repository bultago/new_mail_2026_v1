Profile

You can edit your personal information.

  1. Click [Options > Profile].
  2. Enter your Password in Profile.
  3. Click Check Auth.
  4. Edit your personal information.
    • Basic Info - Edit your personal information including your photo and password.
    • My Info - Edit your information related to your address as well as telephone number.
    • Company Info - Edit your company information.
  5. Click Save once you finish editing the information.
