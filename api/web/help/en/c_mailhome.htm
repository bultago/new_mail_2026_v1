Manage My Mail Home

 

You can modify My Mail Home page which you can see right after you sign in.

 
Manage My Mail Home

To modify My Mail Home, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [Options > My Mail Home Layout].
  2. Select My Mail Home Page among My Mail Home, Function, Inbox, Manage folder.
    • My Mail Home – Portlets will be displayed as you designated.

    [NOTE]
    Portlet list is managed by Administrator in [Services> Domains> Webmail Settings> My Mail Home] in admin page.

    • Function – Function menu will be displayed when you sign in.
    • Inbox - Message list will be displayed when you sign in.
    • Manage Folder - Goes to Folder Management when you sign in.
  3. Click Save once you finish setting.
  4. A completion message will be prompted. Click OK.

[NOTE]
You can check My Mail Home after sign out and sign in again.
