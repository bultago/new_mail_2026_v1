Write Posts

You can create new posts as well as reply to them or make comments.

  • Write new posts
  • Reply to posts
  • Make comments on posts

Write New Posts

To create a new post, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [BBS] on top and select a bulletin board.
  2. Click Write in Basic tab menu.
  3. Create a post which has following items.
    • Subject, Content, Attachment

    [NOTE]
    Virus scan for attachment files carry out when attachment file is not large and Activation of Virus Scan in [Filters > Virus] is enabled. As long as the size of attachemt file is below Max File Size to Scan in [Filters > Virus], virus scan can be done.

  4. Click Post in Basic tab menu once you finish writing it.
  5. The confirmation message will be prompted. Click OK.
  6. The completion message will be prompted. Click OK.

Reply to Posts

You can reply to posts. If you reply to a post, the message will be displayed with the icon() below the post you chose.

  1. Click [BBS] on top and select a bulletin board.
  2. Click the subject of a post you'd like to reply.
  3. Click Reply in Basic tab menu.
  4. Click Post in Basic tab menu once you finish writing it.
  5. The confirmation message will be prompted. Click OK.
  6. The completion message will be prompted. Click OK.

 
Modify Posts

You are able to edit posts that you've created. Or, administrator can edit any posts.

  1. Click [BBS] on top and select a bulletin board.
  2. Click the subject of a post you'd like to edit.
  3. Click Modify in Basic tab menu.
  4. Click Update in Basic tab menu once you finish editing it.
  5. The confirmation message will be prompted. Click OK.
  6. The completion message will be prompted. Click OK.

 
Delete Posts

You are able to delete posts that you've created. Or, administrator can delete any posts.

  1. Click [BBS] on top and select a bulletin board.
  2. Click the subject of a post you'd like to delete. Click Delete in Basic tab menu.
    Or, check posts that you'd like to delete and click Delete in Basic tab menu.
  3. The confirmation message will be prompted. Click OK.
  4. The completion message will be prompted. Click OK.

 
Comments on Posts

You can make comments on posts and read the posts and comments at once.

Make Comments

To make comments, follow the steps below.

  1. Click [BBS] on top and select a bulletin board.
  2. Click the subject of a post you'd like to comment on.
  3. Make a comment and click Post.
    • Comment can be made within 600 bytes.

Delete Comments

You can delete comments that you made. Or, administrator can delete any comments.

  1. Click [BBS] on top and select a bulletin board.
  2. Click the subject of a post.
  3. Click Delete.
