This describes how to search for posts.

 

Search Posts

Search for posts with query terms.

  1. Click [BBS] on top and select a search condition.
    • Subject, Content, Author, Filename, Subject+Content
  2. Enter a query term.
  3. Click Search.

 
My Posts

Search for posts that you've created.

  1. Click [BBS] on top and click My Posts.
  2. Search results will be displayed.
